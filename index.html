glommerZ helps you find free food with no moral questions

Let's say you want some food but you don't feel like paying. You let glommerZ help you find it by specifying your zip code and the day you want to find free food.
glommerZ will point you to a list of places to get free food in your locale and give you tips for getting the food.

Let's say you pick free food given by a rodeo association. We'll tell you that you don't have to wear jeans and that you can mention you love Tennessee Walking Horses. We'll point you to a Wikipedia entry where you can learn enough to show people you're not that much into rodeos but you could pass. Okay now you are in. Scarf the food and just make a beeline for the door.

Don't tell the people giving the food that you're here from glommerZ or the free food will stop for all.
